Mapping Dreams

Mapping Dreams is an experimental projection mapping project that explores how soft objects and surfaces can become immersive visual environments. Dream-like animated visuals were projected onto pillows, transforming familiar domestic objects into surreal, atmospheric landscapes.

Tools Used: TouchDesigner, Projection Mapping

Focus: Immersive visuals, experimental projection, dream-like environments
